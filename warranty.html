Hybrid Battery Warranty Options

We stand behind our work. Whether you choose a basic rebuild or a complete replacement with new modules, every battery repair from Hybrid Battery Hub comes with a warranty tailored to your vehicle and budget.

Our Warranty Plans

We offer a range of warranty plans to ensure peace of mind for every customer. All warranties include coverage for materials and workmanship.

  • 3‑Year New Modules Rebuild Warranty: Long‑term coverage with brand‑new battery modules. Contact us for pricing.

For models such as the Camry, Avalon or other hybrids, we can tailor warranty packages to fit your needs. Reach out to discuss which option is best for you.
